Don't lose your Budget planner data
 
On the 15th of January we'll be waving goodbye to the short Budget planner.
 
That means any data you've saved will disappear, but you still have a bit of time to download it as a spreadsheet.
 
Once you've done that you can manually add it to the new Budget planner for a better overview of your finances.
How do I download my data?
 
Head to the summary page of the short Budget planner and select the 'Download spreadsheet' option from the 'Do more with your budget' menu.
 
 
Once you've got your spreadsheet, you need to copy and paste your information into the new Budget planner.
