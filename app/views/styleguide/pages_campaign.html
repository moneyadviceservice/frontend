Interest rates

When interest rates rise, make sure you don't end up in a financial hole

Interest rates are likely to rise, maybe later this year or perhaps in 2015. The rises may be small and gradual, and they’ll affect everyone with a mortgage. But new research from the Money Advice Service shows three out of four homeowners haven’t considered how a rise would affect their repayments.

More than half of homeowners would find it hard to cover an increase of £150 a month, while three out of four would struggle to pay an extra £200 a month.

But there are actions you can take today so you can be better prepared when the rises come.
